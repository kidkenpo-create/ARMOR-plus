PART 218-EMERGENCY ACQUISITIONS

SUBPART 218.70-AVAILABLE ACQUISITION FLEXIBILITIES


218.7001  Additional acquisition flexibilities.
Additional acquisition flexibilities are available to DoD when supporting emergency acquisitions.

218.7001-1  General emergency flexibilities.
See the list of flexibilities at PGI 218.7001-1.

218.7001-2  Contingency operation.
See the list of flexibilities at PGI 218.7001-2.

218.7001-3  Defense or recovery from certain events.
For acquisitions that, as determined by the head of the contracting activity, are to facilitate defense against or recovery from cyber, nuclear, biological, chemical, or radiological attack; to facilitate provision of international disaster assistance; or to support response to an emergency or major disaster, see the list of flexibilities at PGI 218.7001-3.

218.7001-4  Emergency declaration or major disaster declaration.
See the list of flexibilities at PGI 218.7001-4.

218.7001-5  Humanitarian or peacekeeping operation.
See the list of flexibilities at PGI 218.7001-5.

218.7001-6  Head of contracting activity determinations.
See PGI 218.7001-6 for delegations of authority.

218.7001-7  Use of electronic business tools.
When supporting a contingency operation or humanitarian or peacekeeping operation, follow the procedures at PGI 218.7001-7 concerning the use of electronic business tools.
